About Me

Sahil Sran is an animator and storyboard artist based in Vancouver, Canada. He studied at the School of Interactive Art and Technology at Simon Fraser University and is interested in taking up an art direction role in the video game industry. Throughout his academia, he has pursued 2D animation as a hobby, and hopes to apply those skills to a story-driven game that expresses creativity and a message that resonates with players.
